Jenna Kim

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Boston, advised by Prof. Daniel Haehn. I previously studied Marketing and Business Analytics, bringing a data-driven perspective to my research.

I focus on deep-learning based medical image segmentation with uncertainty estimation for clinically interpretable AI in vascular and cardiac applications. My work bridges methodological advances with real world biomedical applications.

I collaborate with clinicians at the University of Pennsylvania, including Dr. Jae W. Song, and previously worked as an AI Developer at myotwin.
